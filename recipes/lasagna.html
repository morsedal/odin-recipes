Lasagna

The best Lasagna recipe ever!

Honestly, the best part about making the lasagna is eating it afterward. This recipe is very straight forward and can be done fairly quickly. Don't beieve me? Check out the recipe for more information!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef and Italian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes/2 cans of tomato sauce/ and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices/Seasonings(ie. parsley, dried basil, salt, Italian Seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper)
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses(ie. Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta)
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
